Introduction

RealPro is CoGenTex's cross-platform, high performance syntactic realizer. Written entirely in portable JAVA, RealPro is designed to perform syntactic realization -- i.e, the transformation of abstract syntactic specifications of natural language sentences (or phrases) into their corresponding surface forms -- at speeds suitable for interactive processing.

RealPro's syntactic specifications are based on the Deep-Syntactic Structures (DSyntS(s)) of Meaning-Text Theory (see Background on Syntactic Dependency). DSyntSs may be passed as input to RealPro in two ways:

  • either programmatically (see RealPro API, and RealPro Application Samples);

  • or textually using one of two supported file formats (see Representing DSyntS Using DSS Formalism and Representing DSyntS Using XML Formalism).
